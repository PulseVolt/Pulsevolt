PulseVolt

Group 10119 | STEM High School for Boys, 6th of October

Empowering Hospitals with Sustainable Urban Wind Energy

Project Overview

Pulse Volt addresses Egypt's Grand Challenges by introducing a highly efficient approach to renewable energy in urban environments. We utilize a Lenz2 vertical axis wind turbine equipped with a Convergent Omnidirectional Nozzle Guide (CONG) to efficiently generate sustainable electricity. Our mission is to alleviate the stress on national power grids and provide an uninterrupted, clean power supply to critical facilities.

The Problem

Power Instability in Hospitals

Aging city grids and frequent municipal power load-shedding pose direct threats to high-risk demographics, such as intensive care patients and neonatal infants. Sudden outages turn a hospital's reliance on power into a matter of life or death.

Climate Change & Emissions

Relying heavily on backup diesel generators during power failures is costly and results in the emission of harmful fumes. The burning of fossil fuels for conventional electricity continues to drive climate change and ecosystem degradation in Egypt.

Our Solution

Lenz2 Wind Turbine

We selected the Lenz2 geometry for its high torque capabilities and its ability to accept wind from any direction, making it highly effective even amidst chaotic urban wind flows.

CONG Technology

The addition of the Convergent Omnidirectional Nozzle Guide (CONG) forces incoming air to pull faster through the turbine blades, overcoming low-speed wind barriers and maximizing electrical output.

Urban Feasibility & Storage

The compact footprint makes this ideal for rooftop installations. Coupled with a reliable battery storage system, Pulse Volt ensures power can be safely stored and delivered when emergency load-shedding occurs.

Prototype Specifications

  • Scale & Dimensions: 1:1 scale application engineered to stay strictly within a 0.25 m³ volume. The turbine features a height of 1.0 m and a total diameter of 0.45 m.
  • Advanced Aerodynamics: The blades incorporate a 60-degree helical twist along the vertical axis to eliminate "dead spots" and ensure constant torque.
  • Premium Materials: Constructed using 0.5 mm lightweight aluminum sheets for high strength-to-weight ratio, integrated with laser-cut MDF structural ribs.
  • Power Electronics: Equipped with a JGA25-370 DC gear motor acting as a generator, a 50V 0.0033F electrolytic capacitor for storage, and a 5V USB step-down buck converter to safely regulate final output.
